SUSHI ROLLS

Sushi rolls are a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice, various ingredients like raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits, all rolled in seaweed sheets.

Served with soy sauce and wasabi, sushi rolls offer a delightful blend of textures and flavors.

PAD THAI

Originating from Thailand, Pad Thai is a stir-fried noodle dish featuring rice noodles, tofu or shrimp, bean sprouts, peanuts, and lime. The combination of sweet, sour, and spicy flavors makes Pad Thai a beloved and flavorful street food.
